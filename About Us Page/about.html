Our Vision

To be the leading global provider of luxurious vehicles, setting the standard for excellence in service, quality, and innovation.

Our Mission

  • Deliver Unmatched Quality

    To offer a curated selection of the world’s most prestigious automotive brands, ensuring every vehicle meets the highest standards of luxury and performance.

  • Customer-Centric Approach

    To provide an exceptional buying experience characterized by personalized service, timely delivery, and unwavering commitment to safety.

  • Innovative Growth

    To continuously expand our reach and enhance our services through innovation, maintaining our position as a market leader in the luxury automotive industry.

Our History

Founded in 1998, DiamondCut Motors began with a singular vision: to redefine the luxury car market in Indonesia. Over the past two decades, we have grown exponentially, earning a reputation for excellence and becoming the go-to choice for discerning car enthusiasts and successful individuals.

Key Milestones

  • 1998

    DiamondCut Motors is founded, establishing our first showroom in Jakarta.

  • 2005

    Expansion into Surabaya and Bandung, broadening our reach within Indonesia.

  • 2010

    Introduction of prestigious brands like Porsche, Mercedes-Benz, and Bugatti to our lineup.

  • 2015

    Launch of our first nationwide marketing campaign, significantly increasing brand awareness.

  • 2020

    Recognition as Indonesia’s top luxury car reseller, celebrated for our cutting-edge service and customer satisfaction.

  • 2023

    Announcing our vision for global expansion, aiming to bring our unparalleled service to an international clientele.
